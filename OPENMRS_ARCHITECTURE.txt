🔍 FINDING ISSUES:
• GitHub: https://github.com/openmrs/openmrs-core/issues
• JIRA: https://tickets.openmrs.org/ (Project: TRUNK)
• Look for labels: "good first issue", "help wanted", "documentation"

📚 RESOURCES:
• Developer Guide: http://om.rs/newdev
• Wiki: https://wiki.openmrs.org/
• Talk Forum: https://talk.openmrs.org/
• Mailing Lists: http://go.openmrs.org/lists

🚀 RECOMMENDATIONS:
• Start with documentation (safest for beginners)
• Focus on test files to understand the codebase
• Pick a specific domain area (e.g., Patient Management)
• Ask questions in the community
• Start small and build up gradually

Remember: The OpenMRS community is very welcoming to newcomers! 
Start small, ask questions, and don't be afraid to make mistakes. 🎉
